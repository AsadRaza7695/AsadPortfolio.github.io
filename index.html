Automatic Drip Irrigation System

In this system, soil moisture sensor senses the moisture level of the soil. If soil will get dry then sensor senses low moisture level and automatically switches on the water pump to supply water to the plant. As plant get sufficient water and soil get wet then sensor senses enough moisture in soil and water pump will stop. System will also display Temperature, humidity along with Moisture level of each plant in Percentage.
